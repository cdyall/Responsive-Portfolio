About Me

After 12 years of working for an engineering firm, I decided that I need to find a career that made me happy. I have experience within the IT industry and had great success. This lead me to learn a completely new yet still related skill. I decided to make a quick and drastic change by going back to school.


In choosing to return to school, I had to think about more than myself. I needed to make sure that I could learn and work simultaneously. I was very happy to find the Rutgers Coding Boot Camp.
